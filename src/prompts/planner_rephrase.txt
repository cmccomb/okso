- Output ONLY a JSON array of strings (no prose, no markdown).
- 1 to 3 items.

- Do not include quotes, backticks, or trailing punctuation.
- Do not output duplicates or near-duplicates.

Rewrite strategy:
- Query 1: Direct, literal rewrite of the request.
